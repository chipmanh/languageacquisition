technology

flipgird
Uses: question/answer videos in the target language, oral exams
Theories: audiolingual, communicative
Advantages: review, hear every student speak, online community/resources for student
Disadvantages: does not allow for fast corrections, internet access needed, time needed to learn how to use the technology
Lesson Plan: Fourth Hour Assignment where students watch a movie in target language outside of class and upload a video of themself discussing the movie as well as respond to other students' videos
